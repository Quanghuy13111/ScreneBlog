3. The Power of Community: Sharing knowledge is the best way to learn. I'm always open to connecting with and helping other developers.

Beyond the Code

When I'm not at my keyboard, you can find me exploring new places with my camera, getting lost in a good book, or watching a football match. My idol is Cristiano Ronaldo and my favorite club is Real Madrid. I believe that creativity and inspiration can be found everywhere.

A Glimpse of Life

Beyond the code, I live in Ho Chi Minh City, a city that never sleeps. I find balance by waking up early, enjoying the fresh morning air with a cup of coffee, and planning my day. For me, programming is not just a job; it's the art of problem-solving and creating value.
